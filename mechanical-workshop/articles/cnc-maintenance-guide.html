CNC Machine Tool Daily Maintenance Guide

Author: Sarah Liu | Category: Machine Tools and Tooling Machinery | Published: 2026-06-05

1. Overview

CNC Machine Tools and Tooling Machinery are indispensable precision equipment in modern manufacturing. Their daily maintenance is crucial for ensuring machining accuracy and extending equipment service life. This article will detail the various maintenance essentials for CNC Machine Tools and Tooling Machinery.

2. Spindle System Maintenance

The spindle is the core component of CNC Machine Tools and Tooling Machinery, and its performance directly affects machining quality.

2.1 Spindle Lubrication

Regularly check the oil level and oil quality of the spindle lubrication system to ensure sufficient and clean lubricating oil. It is usually recommended to change the spindle lubricating oil every 2000 hours and clean the lubrication system.

2.2 Spindle Temperature Monitoring

Monitor the spindle temperature during operation. If abnormal temperature rise is detected, stop the machine immediately for inspection to investigate issues such as bearing wear or poor lubrication.

3. Lubrication System Maintenance

The lubrication system of CNC Machine Tools and Tooling Machinery includes spindle lubrication, guideway lubrication, ball screw lubrication and other parts.

3.1 Guideway Lubrication

Check the oil level in the guideway lubrication tank before starting the machine every day to ensure the oil level is within the specified range. Regularly clean chips and debris from the guideway surface to keep it clean.

3.2 Ball Screw Lubrication

Lubrication of ball screws is crucial for ensuring transmission accuracy. Regularly check the lubrication status of ball screws and replenish grease if necessary.

4. Cooling System Inspection

The cooling system is responsible for dissipating heat generated during machining, extending tool life and ensuring machining accuracy.

4.1 Coolant Inspection

Regularly check the coolant level and concentration to keep the coolant clean. It is recommended to change the coolant every 3-6 months and clean the cooling system.

4.2 Cooling Pump Maintenance

Check the working status of the cooling pump to ensure no leakage and smooth operation. Regularly clean impurities and sediment from the cooling water tank.

5. Pneumatic System Maintenance

The pneumatic system provides power for machine tool clamping, tool change and other functions, and needs regular inspection.

5.1 Air Source Treatment

Regularly drain water, clean filters, and check if the pressure regulator pressure is normal. It is recommended to drain water from the air tank and filters once a week.

5.2 Cylinder Inspection

Check if each cylinder moves flexibly and if there is any air leakage. If abnormal, replace the seal or cylinder assembly in a timely manner.

6. Electrical System Inspection

6.1 Control Cabinet Cleaning

Regularly clean dust inside the electrical control cabinet and check if each terminal is loose. A comprehensive inspection is recommended every six months.

6.2 Servo System Inspection

Check the working status of servo motors and drives to ensure good heat dissipation and no abnormal noise.

7. Safety Precautions

1. Turn off the machine power and hang a "Under Maintenance" sign before maintenance.

2. Use special tools when cleaning chips to avoid direct contact with rotating parts.

3. Select the oil type recommended by the manufacturer when changing lubricating oil.

4. Record any abnormal conditions discovered during maintenance and contact professional maintenance personnel.

8. Summary

Daily maintenance of CNC Machine Tools and Tooling Machinery is a systematic task that requires the cooperation of operators and maintenance personnel. By establishing a sound maintenance system and regularly checking various indicators, the service life of the equipment can be effectively extended, and processing efficiency and product quality can be improved.
