Community Guidelines

Last updated: January 15, 2024

Welcome to Our Community

Mechanical Workshop is a professional community for machinery enthusiasts, technicians, and industry professionals. These guidelines help ensure our platform remains a safe, respectful, and productive environment for everyone.

Code of Conduct

Be Respectful

Treat others with kindness and respect. We expect all members to communicate professionally and avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

Share Accurate Information

When sharing technical information, specifications, or advice, ensure it is accurate and reliable. Misrepresenting equipment parameters, safety procedures, or technical data can have serious consequences and will not be tolerated.

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Avoid posting unsolicited advertisements, promotional content, or repetitive messages. Commercial posts must be relevant to the machinery industry and follow our advertising guidelines.

Protect Intellectual Property

Respect copyright laws and intellectual property rights. Do not share copyrighted materials (including manuals, images, or software) without proper authorization.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on Mechanical Workshop:

  • Hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory content
  • Violent or harmful content
  • False or misleading information about machinery or equipment
  • Illegal activities or instructions
  • Pornographic or adult content
  • Unauthorized sharing of copyrighted materials
  • Spam, scams, or phishing attempts

Safety First

Always emphasize safety when discussing machinery operation or maintenance. Never encourage unsafe practices or procedures that could result in injury or property damage.

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately using our contact form or email us at moderation@mechanicalworkshop.com. Our moderation team will review reports and take appropriate action.

Consequences of Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning from moderators
  • Content removal
  • Temporary or permanent account suspension

Contact Us

If you have questions about these guidelines or need clarification, please contact us at community@mechanicalworkshop.com.
